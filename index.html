About

Navajivana Higher Secondary school Perdala is a prestigious educational institution established in 1944 by the Perdala educational society, Badiadka, under the able leadership of veteran educationalist as well as a social worker Dr.P.S.Sasthri.It is one of the oldest schools in Kerala. More than 1750 pupils are studying in Kannada, Malayalam and English mediums from fifth to tenth standard.The govt. Of Kerala upgraded this institution into a higher secondary school in 2010.
